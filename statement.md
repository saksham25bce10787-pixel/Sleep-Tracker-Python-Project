# Problem Statement
Many people struggle to maintain a consistent sleep schedule and monitor their sleep quality. There is a need for a simple tool that helps users track their sleep duration and quality, analyze patterns, and get insights for better sleep management.

# Scope
The Sleep Tracker application allows users to record their sleep and wake times, calculate total sleep duration, rate sleep quality, and maintain a history of sleep patterns. The scope includes personal use for health monitoring and can be extended for research or integration with wearable devices.

# Target Users
- Students, professionals, and anyone who wants to monitor and improve their sleep habits.
- People experiencing irregular sleep schedules or sleep-related issues.
- Health enthusiasts and wellness app users.

# High-level Features
1. Input sleep and wake times.
2. Calculate total sleep duration.
3. Record sleep quality.
4. Maintain and display sleep history.
